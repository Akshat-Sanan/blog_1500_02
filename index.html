Hyperion XP-1 Is a 221-MPH Hydrogen-Powered Hypercar

Hyperion XP-1 10th November 2022

Finally a real working prototype of the ever hyped and talked hydrogen car is here, the Hyperion XP-1. Made my Hyperion Motors, it is a 221-MPH Hydrogen-Powered Hypercar, which could go from zero to 60 mph in 2.2 seconds
